Make
====

Makefile Syntax
---------------

    target [target ...]: [component ...]
    [<TAB>command 1]
           .
           .
           .
    [<TAB>command n]


    MACRO = definition

    NEW_MACRO = $(MACRO)-$(MACRO2)

    YYYYMMDD  = ` date `

From command line
-----------------

    make MACRO="value" [MACRO="value" ...] TARGET [TARGET ...]

Internal macros
---------------

    $?
    $@
    $<
    $^
