Prerequisites
-------------

* The AVR tool chain. See AvrBuild.
* Perl YAML module <http://search.cpan.org/~ingy/YAML-0.80/lib/YAML.pm>.
* The Ardunio IDE.
* The Arduino Makefile <https://github.com/mjoldfield/Arduino-Makefile>.
