SatelliteComm
=============

Info on different satellite communication systems.

Iridium
-------

Satellite phones and SDB (Short data burst) modems. (Slow speed)

Inmarsat
--------

Cheap handset Inmarsat satellite phone IsatPhone. Slow with only 2400bps data over satellite.

Thuraya
-------

Satellite phones and Thuraya Module (not for consumers).

* Short message service
* 9.6 kbit/s data
* 60 kbit/s down and 15 kbit/s up 

ORBCOMM
-------

High latency service focused on M2M applications.
